It Hurts

As I lie on my bed instantly I remember the love I had for my wife. It was wholly from the bottom of my heart and my entire being. It hurts so bad. Miss her extremely. Its painful, Im a complete mess and Im crying. What do I do with this unrealised love. How do I continue. One step at time. Write, express my pain openly. Dont hide it anymore. Its almost unbearable, Im struggling second by second. How? Just how? I dont even know where to start. Broken and falling apart. Why does this hurt so much? Why dont I hate her? It hurts, it hurts, it hurts. Man I wish I didnt hurt this much. I wish things were different. But they are not. And now I got to step up and be myself more than ever. This is the worst experience of my life. Its sickening to know how badly she has hurt me. It was stupid to think she was different. I let my guard down and got burnt. I changed aspects of myself and it was never good enough. Why am I never good enough. Why does it always end in heart break. My insides hurt, I feel cold. I feel unwanted. I feel betrayed. I gave all of myself to her. But it was not enough. I gave up everything bar my life even though I tried. Im tired of hurting. Im tired of giving everything and not receiving the same in return. Im tired of being used up and discarded. Im tired of being hurt.
