When God Fights

21st September 2020

Today I was dealing with more attacks from the unseen. I have my two boys staying, they go home tonight. The attacks start 3 days before they arrive, 3 days after, 2 days at random and 1 day for in no particular time frame for no reason. Either way I'm still standing, why? Jesus is the why.

Seeking Salvation From The Enemy

When you live and breathe Jesus, all your faults, your mistakes and your past is used to bring you back to sin. My past is Chaotic at best, but it is just my past. I have been forgiven for all I did badly. Satan on the other hand wants me to remain there. Why? Because he wants me not to speak about my God, and how he saved me. He wants me to remain isolated from others so that I can't speak. Everyday the lords armies battle before my waking eyes just on the edge of my perception. I have always seen demons for what they are, the illusions of the Fallen agents of discord. I can see them as clearly as I see you. Its not a nice site. The Light side are different than what you would expect. Some are heavily armored while others are wearing an assortment of garments. I watch as they repeated drive back attack after attack. The weakest link is not the lords armies but the human general whose mind is in turmoil. Jesus always just appears in blinding light standing in front of me rebuking back the onslaught. It's comforting to know when I'm not relying on him, he steps in and reminds me. Thats when satan loses and runs.

Jesus is the way. Only through him do I win anything. I get all things from christ our god. There is much I have learned. In Christ I can forgive everything. Because of Jesus I can let go of it all.

I read my bible every moment I have free, Why? I am keeping the unspoken covenant I made with christ on my day of salvation, the reaffirming of it on my wedding day and the reaffirming I made at separation. Keeping my covenants with Jesus will keep me separate from sin. I will not remarry another woman or even date. That was a I made my wife in front of God. I intend to keep. I will keep his laws, not the laws of man who have hardened their hearts. For it is said in malachi that God hates divorce and I must honor my wife of my youth. There is no exception, divorce is man-made, it is not from God. Jesus explains this when speaking to the pharasees. Satan wants me to break this covenant. I will not. Divorce hurts for generations, not just the two people involved.
