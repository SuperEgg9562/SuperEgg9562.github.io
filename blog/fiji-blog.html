Fiji Missions Trip 2018

25 April 2018

Fiji Missions Day 1

For the first time in my life, I left New Zealand and headed for Fiji along with 9 other members of the missions team. It was amazing to follow God to help those that needed him. I never felt so right that I was where God planned for me to be. I feel so blessed to be a disciple of God.

Gods Plan For Us

We left New Zealand, for Fiji to help a ministry rebuild a small steel framed house. But on top of that God taught us many things about each other that we would need to be able to complete such a complex endeavour. All of us brought unique practical skills. But we also unknowingly had ministry skills and empathic abilities that would help us mature in our faith with one another and help us reach those around us who might otherwise miss out on Gods Vision for humanity. We were here to increase the effectiveness of God's ministries through our various god-given skils.

Fiji

What a beautiful country. What beautiful people. What a beautiful culture.
