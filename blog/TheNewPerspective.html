What Now?

Now What? Do I go the complete opposite of the norm? Do I study and become my dream? What I do know is Im at a turning point in my life and I can go a number of ways. First up is facing my past and moving one step foward. I have been hurt beyond words to explain thoroughly. Its a hard pill to swallow realising that my once best friend could deal such a dangerous blow. Its like having your insides ripped apart by an immense destructive internal force. And for what was the purpose of hurting me so bad that in my mind death was the only way to escape the pain felt? All or nothing. This has been my approach for as long as I can remember. Im not sure why it seemed to me there was only two ways to go. I do know there is more than two ways. There is an in-between. I can choose feel a certain way. I have free choice. I am the one who controls how I feel, so the things that happen out of my control are not my responsibility unless I make them mine. So to be hurt for so long was a choice I had made unwittingly. I miss my two boys. I think this will as hard as make it.
