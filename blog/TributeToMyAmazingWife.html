For my amazing Wife

Although I said many dangerous words toward you. My love has never changed.

Although I can not change the past. I still choose to love you.

My words may have harmed you. But they will never rule you.

My actions may not have lined up with the word. But GOD will change them.

I hurt you and there is no excuse. I was wrong. I was weak.

I am not without blemish, in truth I am covered in them.

When I said you were my sunshine, I meant it.

When I said my vows I meant EVERY ONE.

Even in bad times I choose you.

Even now when hope is but a distant light, I choose you.
